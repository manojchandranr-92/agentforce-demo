## Story

> As a leasing manager, I want the lead to be linked to a specific apartment only when the visitor clearly expresses interest in it, so that my team knows exactly which unit to follow up about.

## Acceptance criteria

- Apartment ID is passed to lead creation only when the visitor explicitly identifies a specific unit (e.g. 'I am interested in Unit 1051', 'Please schedule a tour for Financial District')
