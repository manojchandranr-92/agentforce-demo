# US-04 — Lead Interest Level Classification

- **Priority:** high
- **Status:** backlog
- **Epic:** Apartment Search & Lead Capture

## Story

> As a leasing manager, I want every new lead to be tagged with an interest level (High, Medium, or Low), so that my sales team knows how to prioritize follow-up.

## Acceptance criteria

- High interest: move-in within 7 days; requests a tour; requests booking or lease application; requests immediate follow-up
- Medium interest: move-in within 30 days; requests additional apartment details; pricing clarification; tour at a later date
- Low interest: browsing only; no timeline; no tour/booking request; declines follow-up; ends conversation after viewing
- Interest level is determined from the full conversation before lead creation
- If a visitor ends the conversation after seeing results — regardless of prior signals — the level is always classified as Low
- The classified level is stored on the Lead record
